Cellular Network Biology

The JensenLab develops state-of-the-art tools for generation and analysis of molecular interaction networks from proteomics data and text mining, which we make freely available to the scientific community.

Our research group is part of the NNF Center for Protein Research, and is located at the University of Copenhagen, Denmark. If you are interested in joining the group as a PhD student, have a look at the Copenhagen Bioscience PhD Programme.
